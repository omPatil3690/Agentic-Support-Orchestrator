Title: Invoice Not Received by User

Users may report that they did not receive an invoice
after completing a successful payment.

Common reasons:
- Invoice email was sent to spam or junk folder
- Incorrect billing email address
- Delay in invoice generation

Steps to resolve:
1. Verify the user's payment status
2. Confirm the billing email address
3. Regenerate the invoice if required
4. Send the invoice to the registered billing email
5. Provide instructions to download invoices from the user dashboard

Important notes:
- Invoices should only be sent to verified billing emails
- Do not share invoices containing sensitive information in chat
