Title: Subscription Cancellation Request

Users may request to cancel their active subscription
to stop future billing.

Common reasons:
- No longer using the service
- Pricing concerns
- Temporary requirement

Steps to resolve:
1. Verify the user's account and subscription status
2. Confirm the cancellation request
3. Cancel the subscription through the billing system
4. Inform the user about access validity until the billing period ends
5. Explain refund eligibility if applicable

Important notes:
- Cancellation stops future billing but does not always trigger a refund
- Access usually remains active until the current billing cycle ends
