Title: Refund Policy and Processing Time

Refunds are issued when a user cancels a subscription
or when an eligible payment dispute is approved.

Refund conditions:
- Refunds are available within the first 7 days of subscription
- Refunds are not applicable after the billing cycle ends
- Partial refunds are not supported unless stated otherwise

Steps to resolve refund requests:
1. Verify the user's payment and subscription details
2. Check if the refund request falls within the eligible period
3. Initiate the refund through the billing system
4. Inform the user about the expected processing time

Processing time:
- Refunds typically take 3 to 5 business days
- Bank processing times may vary

Important notes:
- Do not promise instant refunds
- Refund eligibility must be verified before initiation
